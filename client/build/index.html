Lobby · Task setup

Real tasks. Real rooms.

Custom tasks, Quiplash style. In the lobby every player adds tasks for your specific space — your kitchen, your couch, your weird hallway closet. Anyone can remove a dud. Save the list and reuse it next party.

  • Everyone in the room contributes
  • Tagged to your real locations
  • Save lists, reuse them later

Emergency meeting

Bring it to the table.

Slam the red button and everyone gathers. Make your case, vote, and pray you guessed right. One wrong vote can lose the round.

  • Triggered by any player
  • 30s to discuss and vote
  • Veto, accuse, or eject

Intruder POV

A trick up your sleeve.

Intruders draw sabotage cards mid-game: hack phones, force meetings, fake tasks, mess with the lights. Each card flips the table.

  • Hold a hand of sabotage cards
  • Single-use, high impact
  • Pick off crew between meetings

Optional · Reactor mode

Run a meltdown? Up to you.

Plug a second device into a TV as the reactor terminal. When the meltdown starts, every player gets a personal shutdown code on their phone — they sprint to the terminal and key it in before the timer hits zero.

  • Optional second-screen mode
  • Race to enter shutdown PINs
  • 30s to defuse or you lose

How it works

Three minutes to setup. The rest is mayhem.

  1. 01

    Open susparty.com

    On a laptop or TV for the host, and on every player’s phone. No app, no signup, no downloads.

  2. 02

    Customize tasks for your venue

    “Swap the dish towel.” “Check the basement light.” Make the task list match your house, dorm, or office.

  3. 03

    Sneak. Lie. Vote. Repeat.

    Crewmates rush real-life tasks. Intruders pick people off and stir chaos. Meetings decide who walks the plank.

Quick answers

The stuff people always ask.

Do I need to install an app?
Nope. It runs in your browser. iPhone, Android, laptop. All good.
How many players?
Best with 5–15. Works in a pinch with 4. Gets wild past 10.
Does it cost anything?
No. It’s free, open source, and ad-free forever.
Where do we play?
Anywhere with multiple rooms: a house, apartment, dorm, office after hours, big Airbnb. Wi-Fi helps.
