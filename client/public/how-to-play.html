Game Setup

🏠 Choose Your Location

This game works best in a large house or building with multiple rooms and floors. You need enough space that players can move around without constantly seeing each other.

Ideal Venues Multi-story houses, office buildings, college dorms, or any space with separate rooms and areas where players can spread out and intruders can strike unnoticed!

Meeting Area

REQUIRED

A central location where everyone gathers when a meeting is called. Usually the living room or kitchen.

Core Location

OPTIONAL (Recommended)

Place a laptop in a separate area (basement, attic, garage). Used for meltdown events.

📝 Creating Tasks

Before starting, you'll create a task list - these are real activities players will do around your venue!

Tasks are completed on the honor system! When you finish a task in real life, swipe to mark it complete on your phone. Be honest - it's more fun that way!

Example tasks:

  • 🧹 Sweep the kitchen floor (Kitchen)
  • 🎵 Play a song on the speaker (Living Room)
  • 📝 Write a funny note and hide it (Bedroom)
  • 🥤 Do a shot (Bar Area)
  • 💪 Do 10 jumping jacks (Basement)

Pro tip: You can save your task list and share it with a code! Load it next time or share with friends hosting their own game.

Player Roles

When the game starts, each player is secretly assigned a role. Check your phone to see if you're a Crewmate or an Intruder!

Crewmate

  • Complete tasks around the venue
  • Watch for suspicious behavior
  • Report dead bodies you find
  • Call emergency meetings
  • Vote out who you think is the intruder
  • Stay alive!

Intruder

  • Eliminate crewmates secretly
  • Blend in and fake doing tasks
  • Use ability cards for sabotage
  • Cause chaos with core meltdowns
  • Deceive others during meetings
  • Don't get caught!

Gameplay

👥 For Crewmates

Get Your Task

Your phone shows one task at a time. Go to the location and complete the task in real life.

Swipe to Complete

When you've done the task, swipe on your phone to mark it complete. A new task will appear.

Stay Alert

Watch for suspicious behavior. If you see someone acting weird or find a body, call a meeting!

🔪 For Intruders

Find a Target

Look for isolated crewmates. Make sure no one else is watching!

Eliminate Them

Tap them on the shoulder (or anywhere) and whisper "you're dead" or just let them get the picture. Then swipe the kill button on your phone.

Act Natural

Pretend to do tasks. Walk around purposefully. Build alibis. Lie convincingly in meetings!

When You're Killed

Lay down on the floor and play dead! You cannot speak, move, or use your phone until a meeting is called. Your body can be discovered by other players.

Meetings & Voting

Meetings are called when someone finds a dead body or wants to discuss suspicious activity.

🚨 CRITICAL: Dead Players Must Report!

If you were killed, you MUST press the "I'm Dead" button on your phone BEFORE the meeting starts. This is essential for the game to work properly!

Meeting Flow

Meeting Called

Everyone goes to the designated meeting area. NO TALKING until the meeting officially begins!

Ready Up

Each player presses "Ready" on their phone. Dead players press "I'm Dead" instead.

Discussion (Timed)

Once everyone is ready, discussion begins! Accuse, defend, and debate. The timer is running!

Vote

Vote for who you think is the intruder, or skip if you're unsure. Majority rules!

Results

The player with the most votes is ejected. You'll find out if they were an intruder or not!

Skip Vote / Veto

If enough players vote to skip (veto), no one gets ejected. Use this when you're genuinely unsure - voting out the wrong person helps the intruders!

Core Meltdown

The core is an optional but highly recommended feature that adds chaos and excitement!

⚙️ Setup

Place a laptop or desktop computer in a location separate from the meeting area - ideally somewhere players have to travel to reach (basement, attic, garage, etc.).

💥 When Meltdown is Triggered

  1. All players receive a 4-digit code on their phones
  2. Everyone must rush to the core (laptop)
  3. Enter your code into the core before time runs out
  4. A certain percentage of codes must be entered to stop the meltdown
If the meltdown isn't stopped in time...

The intruders WIN immediately! This creates pressure and forces crewmates to group up, giving intruders opportunities to strike or blend in.

Intruder Ability Cards

Intruders start with special ability cards and can draw more by surviving meetings. These add strategic depth and make intruder gameplay more interesting!

🗣️ Self Report

Call an emergency meeting yourself. Perfect for framing someone else after a kill!

📡 Remote Sabotage

Trigger a core meltdown without being near it. Create chaos from anywhere!

⏱️ Shorten Meltdown

Reduce the time players have to stop the next meltdown, making it harder to save.

🚫 Area Denial

Block a location - crewmates stop getting tasks there. Great after a kill!

⚡ EMP

Disable all player devices for a duration. Creates confusion and panic!

🔊 Taunt

Play spooky sounds through Sonos speakers to freak out the crewmates!

🔄 Discard & Draw

Discard a card and draw a new one. Useful when you have duplicates!

Drawing Cards

Intruders have a chance to draw a new card after surviving each meeting. The longer you stay alive, the more powerful you become!

Win Conditions

🎉 Crewmates Win

  • Complete all tasks before everyone dies
  • Vote out ALL intruders

💀 Intruders Win

  • Kill enough crewmates (equal to intruders)
  • Core meltdown succeeds

Pro Tips

💡 Crewmate Tips

  • Travel in pairs when possible - imposters can't kill with witnesses
  • Note where you see other players - build mental alibis
  • Pay attention to who accuses who in meetings
  • If someone is very quick to blame, they might be deflecting
  • Don't vote someone out unless you have real evidence

🎭 Intruder Tips

  • Don't kill too fast - pace yourself to avoid suspicion
  • Create alibis by being seen in common areas
  • Pretend to do tasks - stand in task locations briefly
  • Use meltdowns to separate groups or create chaos
  • In meetings, ask questions instead of making accusations
  • If another intruder is suspected, sometimes let them go

Sound Effects (Optional)

  • ⚠️ Alarm sounds during meltdowns
  • 🎵 Victory/defeat themes
  • 👻 Spooky taunt sounds (imposter ability)

Check the Sonos Connector instructions in the game settings to set this up!
